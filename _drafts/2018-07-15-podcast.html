I often listen to podcasts, and am sometimes asked to recommend podcasts to others. So, I thought I'd list the podcasts I listen to here. I've broken them down into four categories, based on my listening habits:

High Priority Shows

New episides of these shows jump to the top of my queue, no matter what else is there or what I'm in the middle of:
  • Accidental Tech Podcast - Weekly tech commentary from three of my favorite internet people: Marco Arment, Casey Liss, and John Siracusa. Always interesting, often hilarious.
  • Hello Internet - Hard to describe unless you've listened to it. Frequent topics include: Internet culture, YouTube economics, flags, airplane crashes, and the skyline of Adelaide, Australia.
